## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 User Account Data
- Account information is retained for the duration of the account
- Upon account termination, data is retained for 30 days then deleted (unless legal hold applies)

### 4.2 Lead Information
- Lead information is retained while the account is active
- After account termination, lead information is deleted within 90 days
- Users can request earlier deletion subject to legal holds

### 4.3 Communication History
- Communication records are retained for 24 months for compliance and dispute resolution
- After 24 months, communication history is archived or deleted
- Users can request earlier deletion

### 4.4 Analytics and Log Data
- Server logs are retained for 90 days
- Aggregate analytics are retained indefinitely (cannot identify individuals)

### 4.5 Data Backups
- Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 30 days
- Backups are maintained for disaster recovery purposes only

## 8. AI and Automated Decision-Making

### 8.1 AI-Generated Content
The Service uses artificial intelligence (Claude API via Anthropic) to:

- Analyze lead information
- Provide recommendations
- Optimize communication suggestions

### 8.2 Significant Automated Decisions
