### 4. **TCPA Compliance Checklist** (`04-TCPA-COMPLIANCE-CHECKLIST.md`)
**172 KB | 16 sections**

Comprehensive TCPA compliance guide for SMS marketing:
- ✅ **Consent requirements** (express written consent required)
- ✅ **Opt-out procedures** (STOP reply handling)
- ✅ **Do-Not-Call compliance** (FTC registry + state lists)
- ✅ **Sender identification** (business name, ID, phone, address)
- ✅ **Message content** (no deceptive/false claims)
- ✅ **Timing rules** (8 AM - 9 PM recipient timezone)
- ✅ **AI-generated content** (disclose if AI-generated)
- ✅ **Lead consent documentation** (retention requirements)
- ✅ **SMS provider compliance** (use TCPA-compliant providers)
- ✅ **Real estate specifics** (property-specific consent, open houses)
- ✅ **Complaint handling** (documented procedures)
- ✅ **Enforcement & penalties** ($43,792-$131,376 per violation)
- ✅ **Compliance checklist** (must-do, recommended, do-not-do)

**Violations Covered:**
- No consent requirement
- Ignoring opt-outs
- Sending outside 8 AM-9 PM
- Using spoofed sender ID
- Failing to include business ID
- Re-contacting after opt-out
- Sending to Do-Not-Call list
- TCPA penalties up to $43.8K per violation

**Note:** Phase 1 is SMS/text only (no voice calls). All voice/call features await Phase 2.

## 🔧 Customization Required

### Before Publishing

**Update these fields in all documents:**

1. **Company Name & Contact:**
   - Replace `[Company Name]` with your company name
   - Replace `[domain]` with your domain (legal@[domain], privacy@[domain], etc.)
   - Replace `[Company Address]` with mailing address

2. **Jurisdiction:**
   - Replace `[JURISDICTION]` with your governing law state/country
   - Recommend: Delaware or California (tech-friendly)
   - Note: GDPR/CCPA apply regardless of jurisdiction for EU/CA users

3. **Arbitration (ToS § 9.2):**
   - Replace `[ARBITRATION ORGANIZATION]` with your choice
   - Options: JAMS, AAA, or other arbitration body
   - Recommended: JAMS (tech-experienced arbitrators)

4. **Sub-Processors (DPA § 4):**
   - Update table with your actual providers
   - Get DPAs signed with each sub-processor BEFORE publishing
   - Note: Anthropic (Claude), Twilio, Cloud provider must be confirmed

5. **Insurance Carriers (Insurance § 4):**
   - Add your insurance company name once purchased
   - Add policy number and coverage limits
   - Add effective dates and renewal dates

### Before Going Live

**Legal Review (Recommended):**
- Have a compliance attorney review all 5 documents
- Focus on: AI liability disclaimers, TCPA language, jurisdiction
- Estimated cost: $1,500-3,000 for 4-6 hour review
- Timeline: 1-2 weeks

**Regulatory Check:**
- Verify state-specific laws don't conflict with terms
- Check if real estate industry has specific SMS requirements
- Review consumer protection laws in your target states
