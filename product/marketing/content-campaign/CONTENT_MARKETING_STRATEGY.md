### Primary: Solo Real Estate Agents
- **Pain:** Drowning in leads, can't respond fast enough

- **Content they consume:** Quick tips, success stories, market insights

### Secondary: Small Real Estate Teams
- **Pain:** Lead routing chaos, inconsistent follow-up

- **Content they consume:** Scaling strategies, team management tips

### Tertiary: Real Estate Coaches & Influencers
- **Pain:** Need tools to recommend to their audience
- **Where they hang out:** Podcasts, YouTube, conferences
