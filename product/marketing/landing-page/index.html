Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI know what to say? +

LeadFlow AI is trained on millions of real estate conversations and your specific market data. It sounds natural because it learns from the best-performing agents. You can also customize responses and add your own scripts.

Can I take over a conversation? +

Absolutely. You have full visibility into every conversation through your dashboard. With one click, you can jump in and take over — the AI will step back and let you handle it.

What if a lead asks something the AI can't handle? +

The AI is trained to recognize when it's out of its depth. It'll smoothly transition to you with a message like "Let me have my agent get back to you on that" and immediately notify you.

Do I need to integrate with my existing CRM? +

Not required, but recommended. LeadFlow works out of the box, but integrates seamlessly with popular real estate CRMs like Follow Up Boss, LionDesk, and kvCORE. New integrations added monthly.

Is there a contract or commitment? +

No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime. We also offer a 14-day free trial so you can see results before paying a penny.
