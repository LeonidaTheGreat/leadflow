## 2. Participant Commitments

### During the Pilot, You Agree To:
1. **Use the system** with at least 10 new leads during the 30-day period
2. **Provide feedback** via:
   - Weekly 10-min check-in (Week 1, 2, 3)
   - Final 30-min feedback interview (Week 4)
   - Written feedback form at conclusion
3. **Allow data collection** for product improvement (anonymized)
4. **Be available** for technical support questions

### You Are NOT Required To:
- Pay any fees during the pilot
- Continue after the pilot ends
- Provide a testimonial (though we'd appreciate it!)
- Refer other agents (though we'd love introductions)

## 3. Data and Privacy

### What We Collect
- Lead interaction data (response rates, booking conversions)
- AI conversation logs (for quality improvement)
- Feature usage statistics
- Your feedback and suggestions

### What We DON'T Collect
- Your client's personal financial information
- Transaction details
- Commission data
- Anything not related to lead response

### Data Usage
- All data is used solely for product improvement
- We may use aggregated, anonymized data for marketing
- Individual agent data is never shared publicly without permission
- You can request data deletion at any time
