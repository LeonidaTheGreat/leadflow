# LeadFlow AI - Onboarding Call Script

## Call Overview

**Duration:** 15-20 minutes  
**Goal:** Get agent excited, set up their account, schedule follow-up  
**Platform:** Cal.com (already integrated)  
**Recording:** Ask permission for internal use only

---

## Pre-Call Preparation

### Before the Call:
- [ ] Review agent's profile (location, volume, CRM)
- [ ] Prepare screen share (dashboard ready)
- [ ] Have pilot agreement ready to send
- [ ] Test your audio/video
- [ ] Open agent's website/social for reference

### Materials Ready:
- [ ] Pilot agreement (PDF)
- [ ] Onboarding checklist
- [ ] Support email template
- [ ] FAQ document

---

## Call Structure

### 1. Opening (2 minutes)

**Greeting:**
```
"Hey [Name]! Thanks for taking the time. I'm [Your Name] with LeadFlow AI. 

Really excited to talk to you - I've been following your work in [Market] and love [specific thing from their social/website]."
```

**Set Agenda:**
```
"Here's what we'll cover in the next 15-20 minutes:
• Quick intro to LeadFlow and how it works
• See if it's a fit for your business
• Walk through the simple setup
• Answer any questions
• Get you started if you're ready

Sound good?"
```

**Permission to Record:**
```
"Do you mind if I record this? It's just for our internal team to make sure I'm giving you accurate information. I won't share it anywhere."
```

---

### 2. Discovery (3-4 minutes)

**Understand Their Current Process:**
```
"Before I show you the product, help me understand your current situation:

1. How are you handling lead response right now? Are you able to respond to every lead within 5 minutes?

2. What happens when a lead comes in at 10 PM or while you're showing a home?

3. Roughly how many new leads are you getting per week?

4. What's your biggest frustration with your current lead follow-up process?"
```

**Listen For:**
- Response time pain ("I miss leads while showing homes")
- Work-life balance issues ("I'm on my phone 24/7")
- Lost deals ("Someone else got to them first")
- Time waste ("I spend hours qualifying leads")

**Acknowledge Pain:**
```
"That makes total sense. [Summarize their pain]. That's exactly what we built LeadFlow to solve."
```

---

### 3. Product Demo (5-6 minutes)

**Transition:**
```
"Let me show you how LeadFlow works. I'll share my screen - can you see it?"
```

**Dashboard Walkthrough:**

**A. Lead Flow Overview (1 min)**
```
"Here's how it works:

1. A lead comes in from Zillow/Realtor.com/Facebook
2. LeadFlow AI responds via SMS in under 30 seconds
3. The AI qualifies them through natural conversation
4. When they're ready, it books them directly to your Cal.com calendar
5. Everything syncs back to your Follow Up Boss automatically

You're only involved when the lead is qualified and ready to talk."
```

**B. Live Demo - Agent View (2 min)**
```
"Let me show you what you'd see. This is your dashboard:

[Show dashboard]
• All your leads in one place
• Conversation history with each lead
• See which leads are AI-handled vs need your attention
• Analytics on response times and conversions

[Click into a lead]
• Full conversation history
• Lead qualification score
• Notes from AI conversations
• One-click to take over the conversation"
```

**C. SMS Example (1 min)**
```
"Here's what the actual SMS conversation looks like:

[Show example conversation]

Lead: 'Hi, I saw your listing on Zillow. Is it still available?'
AI: 'Hi there! Yes, 123 Main St is still available. Are you looking to move in the next 30 days or just browsing?'
Lead: 'We're looking to move next month'
AI: 'Great timing! Would you like to schedule a viewing this week? I have openings Tuesday at 2pm or Wednesday at 10am.'
Lead: 'Tuesday works'
AI: [Sends Cal.com link for booking]

Then you get notified in FUB with all the context."
```

**D. Setup Process (1 min)**
```
"The setup is super simple - literally 10 minutes:

1. Connect your Follow Up Boss (2 clicks)
2. Add your Cal.com booking link
3. Customize your AI voice (or use our templates)
4. Test with one lead
5. Turn it on

That's it. No complicated workflows to build."
```

---

### 4. Address Concerns (2-3 minutes)

**Common Objections & Responses:**

**"Will the AI sound robotic?"**
```
"Great question. We've trained it specifically on real estate conversations, so it sounds natural. Plus, you can customize the tone - professional, friendly, whatever fits your brand.

Want to see a sample conversation? [Show example]"
```

**"What if the AI says something wrong?"**
```
"You have full control. You can:
• Set boundaries on what it can discuss
• Review conversations anytime
• Take over manually with one click
• Train it on your specific responses

It's like having an assistant - you're still the boss."
```

**"I don't want to lose the personal touch"**
```
"Totally get that. LeadFlow actually helps you be MORE personal, not less. 

Instead of sending generic 'Thanks for your interest' messages at 11 PM, the AI has a real conversation, qualifies them, and hands them off to you with full context. 

So when you do talk to them, you know exactly what they need. It's actually a better experience for the lead."
```

**"How much does this cost?"**
```
"During the pilot, it's completely free. Zero cost, no credit card.

After the pilot, if you want to continue, pilot participants get 50% off for 3 months - so $75/month instead of $149.

But honestly, if you don't see value in the first 2 weeks, we don't want you to pay. The pilot is truly risk-free."
```

**"What if it doesn't work with my FUB setup?"**
```
"We built this specifically for FUB - it's a true integration, not an add-on. 

It reads your lead data, updates statuses, adds notes, the whole thing. If you can use FUB, this will work with your setup.

And if there ARE any issues, we'll troubleshoot together during the pilot."
```

---

### 5. Pilot Program Details (2 minutes)

**What They Get:**
```
"Here's what the pilot includes:

✅ Full access for 30 days - unlimited leads, unlimited SMS
✅ Full setup support - I'll walk you through it
✅ Weekly check-ins to optimize your setup
✅ Direct access to me for questions
✅ Priority feature requests

All completely free."
```

**What We Ask:**
```
"In exchange, we ask for:
• Use it with at least 10 leads during the month
• 3 short check-in calls (15 mins each)
• Honest feedback - what's working, what's not
• A testimonial at the end if you love it (no obligation)

That's it. No hidden fees, no gotchas."
```

---

### 6. Close & Next Steps (2 minutes)

**Trial Close:**
```
"Based on what you've seen, does this feel like it could solve your lead response problem?"
```

**If YES:**
```
"Awesome! Let's get you started. Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send you the pilot agreement now (just a formality - outlines what we discussed)
2. We can do your setup call as early as tomorrow
3. You'll be live and handling leads within 24 hours

Can you check your email real quick so I can send the agreement?"
```

**Send Agreement:**
```
"Just sent it! It's a simple doc that covers:
• The 30-day free pilot
• What we provide
• What we ask for (the feedback)
• Pricing after pilot (only if you want to continue)

Take a look and let me know if you have any questions. You can sign it electronically."
```

**Schedule Setup Call:**
```
"Once you sign, let's get your setup call scheduled. When works better for you - tomorrow or [day]?"

[Use Cal.com to book the setup call right then]
```

**If They Need to Think About It:**
```
"No problem at all - this is a decision that affects your business, so take your time.

What questions do you still have? What would you need to know to feel confident moving forward?"

[Address concerns]

"Totally understand. How about this - I'll send you the agreement to review, and I'll follow up in a couple days. If you have questions in the meantime, just shoot me a text or email.

Sound fair?"
```

---

### 7. Closing (1 minute)

**Wrap Up:**
```
"Thanks so much for your time today, [Name]. I'm really excited about the possibility of working together.

[If they signed up]: Looking forward to our setup call on [day]. I'll send you a reminder.

[If they need time]: I'll send that agreement over and follow up with you on [day]. Feel free to reach out anytime.

Either way, keep crushing it in [Market]!"
```

## Success Metrics

### Call Success Criteria:
- [ ] Agent understands the product
- [ ] Agent's pain points were acknowledged
- [ ] Demo completed without technical issues
- [ ] Objections addressed satisfactorily
- [ ] Pilot agreement sent
- [ ] Setup call scheduled (or follow-up date set)

### Conversion Targets:
- **Goal:** 60% of calls → agreement signed
- **Goal:** 80% of agreements → onboarding completed
- **Stretch:** 90% of onboarded → pilot completion

---

## Tips for Success

### Do:
- ✅ Ask about THEIR business first
- ✅ Listen more than you talk
- ✅ Use their language ( mirror their words)
- ✅ Show, don't just tell (live demo)
- ✅ Be genuinely helpful, not salesy
- ✅ Follow up promptly

### Don't:
- ❌ Talk about features without context
- ❌ Rush through the demo
- ❌ Be pushy about signing up
- ❌ Badmouth competitors
- ❌ Oversell capabilities
- ❌ Forget to follow up
