## Response Handling

### If They Reply "Interested"
1. Respond within 2 hours
2. Ask 2-3 discovery questions
3. Send Cal.com link to book onboarding call
4. Update tracking sheet

### If They Reply "Tell Me More"
1. Send value-add info (case study, demo video)
2. Offer 10-min call to show product
3. Include Cal.com link

### If They Reply "Not Interested"
1. Thank them for their time
2. Ask what would make it interesting (feedback)
3. Add to nurture list for later

### If They Reply "Too Expensive" (post-pilot)
1. Emphasize free pilot
2. Mention pilot pricing ($75/mo vs $149)
3. Offer ROI calculator
