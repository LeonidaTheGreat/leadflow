# LeadFlow AI — SMS Message Templates

**Product:** AI Lead Response System for Real Estate Agents  
**Platform:** SMS (100–160 characters recommended, support longer with continuation)  
**Tone:** Friendly, professional, action-oriented

---

## Template 1: Initial Response (Qualified Lead)

**Use Case:** Agent responds to a new lead form submission or inquiry within 2–5 minutes.

**Goal:** Qualify quickly, show responsiveness, offer immediate next step.

---

### Option A: Friendly & Direct
```
Hi [Lead Name]! 👋 Thanks for your interest in [Property Name]. 
I'd love to help you find the perfect fit. Are you available for 
a showing today at 2 or 4 PM? Let me know! — [Agent Name]
```
**Length:** 158 characters | **CTA:** Two time options

### Option B: Casual & Warm
```
Hey [Lead Name]! Excited to show you [Property Name]. It's a 
great fit for your needs. Free this afternoon for a quick tour? 
Thinking 2 or 4 PM — which works? — [Agent Name]
```
**Length:** 157 characters | **CTA:** Choose time

### Option C: Professional & Efficient
```
Hello [Lead Name], Thank you for inquiring about [Property Name]. 
I'm available for a private showing today at 2:00 PM or 4:00 PM. 
Reply with your preference. — [Agent Name] | [Phone]
```
**Length:** 159 characters | **CTA:** Select time slot

### Option D: Value-First
```
[Lead Name], you've been matched with a perfect property! 
[Property Address], $[Price]K. I can schedule your private 
showing today at 2 PM or 4 PM. Which time? — [Agent Name]
```
**Length:** 158 characters | **CTA:** Pick time

**Goal:** Re-engage without being pushy, offer alternative format.

---

— [Agent Name]

I can hold a showing slot for you until 5 PM today. Would 
2 PM or 4 PM work? — [Agent Name]

```

📍 Location/Walkability 💰 Price Point 🏠 Space
Reply with your #1 priority and I'll send details. 
— [Agent Name]

He's scheduling a tour today. Still interested in seeing it? 
I can fit you in at 2 or 4 PM. — [Agent Name]
```
**Length:** 153 characters | **CTA:** Social proof + time selection

## Usage Guidelines

### Before Sending

1. **Personalization Checklist**
   - [ ] Lead name correct
   - [ ] Property name/address accurate
   - [ ] Price and details up-to-date
   - [ ] Time slots actually available
   - [ ] Agent name/phone included

2. **Tone Matching**
   - Match lead's communication style (formal ↔ casual)
   - Adjust for lead demographics (if known)
   - Use name after first message to build rapport

3. **Compliance**
   - Include opt-out option: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
   - Respect Do-Not-Call registries
   - Identify as business (legally required in most US states)
   - Follow TCPA guidelines for automated messages

### Response Tracking

| Template | Ideal Response Time | Success Rate |
|----------|-------------------|--------------|
| Initial Response | < 30 min | 45–55% reply rate |
| Follow-up (Nurture) | 30–90 min | 25–35% reply rate |
| Booking Confirmation | Immediate | 90%+ confirmation |
| Handoff | 1–2 hours | 60–70% handoff success |
| Re-engagement | 3–7 days | 15–25% re-engagement |

### A/B Testing Ideas

- **Option A vs B:** Casual tone vs. professional
- **CTA Variants:** "Reply YES" vs. "Pick 1 or 2 PM"
- **Emoji Usage:** With emojis vs. text-only
- **Length:** Short (< 140 chars) vs. detailed (160 chars)
- **Time Pressure:** "Today only" vs. open-ended

### Customization

All templates use placeholders in [Brackets]:
- `[Lead Name]` → First name recommended
- `[Property Name]` → Use nickname ("The Downtown Loft") or address
- `[Property Address]` → Full address or street only
- `[Price]` → Property price in thousands ($450K)
- `[Date]` → Full date ("March 15") or relative ("Today")
- `[Time]` → 12-hour format ("2:00 PM")
- `[Agent Name]` → Your name or alias
- `[Phone]` → Your phone number (optional but recommended)
- `[link]` → Virtual tour, booking link, or property page
- `[Area]` → Neighborhood or zip code

### Advanced Personalization

Templates automatically expand with:
- Buyer preference history (if available)
- Past property interest
- Lead source indicators
- Engagement level/temperature

Example expansion:
```
Hi Sarah! 👋 Thanks for your interest in downtown loft living. 
I noticed you loved mid-century modern. This one has original 
hardwoods + huge windows. Tour today? 2 or 4 PM? — [Agent Name]
```
