# LeadFlow Pilot Recruitment - Outreach Email Templates

## Template 1: Cold Outreach Email

**Subject**: Quick question about {Business Name}'s booking process

Hi {First Name},

I came across {Business Name} while searching for {service type} in {City}, and I noticed you don't have online booking set up yet.

I'm working on a new lead management tool specifically designed for small service businesses like yours—one that captures every inquiry, eliminates no-shows with automated reminders, and tracks where your best leads come from.

We're looking for 3 pilot customers to test it for free for 30 days. No cost, no commitment—just your honest feedback.

Would you be open to a 10-minute call next week to see if it's a fit?

Best,
[Your Name]
LeadFlow Early Access Program

P.S. - Most of our early users say they were losing 2-3 leads per week before switching. Happy to share how we fixed that.

## Usage Notes

### Cold Email Best Practices
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM recipient's local time
- Personalize the first line with something specific about their business
- Keep under 150 words for highest response rates

### Warm Referral Tips
- Always get permission from referrer before name-dropping
- Mention specific results relevant to their industry
- Create urgency with limited pilot spots

### Follow-Up Timing
- First follow-up: 3-4 days after initial email
- Second follow-up: 7 days after first follow-up
- Third follow-up: 14 days later, then move to nurture

### Subject Line Alternatives

**Cold Email Alternatives:**
- "I tried booking with {Business Name}..."
- "Saw your {City} business on Google"
- "Quick idea for {Business Name}"

**Follow-Up Alternatives:**
- "Re: {original subject}"
- "Should I close your file?"
- "One last try, {First Name}"
