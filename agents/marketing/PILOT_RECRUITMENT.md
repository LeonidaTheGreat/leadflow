## 🎯 Screening Questions

Ask these before accepting a pilot user:

1. **"How many leads do you typically get per week?"**
   - Ideal: 10-30
   - Accept: 5-50
   - Decline: <5 (not enough data) or 50+ (too much for MVP)

2. **"What CRM or lead management system do you use?"**
   - Ideal: Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, LionDesk
   - Accept: Any major CRM with webhook/API
   - Decline: Spreadsheets or no system

3. **"Walk me through your current lead response process."**
   - Listen for: Manual response, slow times, frustration
   - Red flags: Already has perfect system, not willing to change

4. **"What's your biggest frustration with lead response right now?"**
   - Ideal: "Missing weekend leads," "Can't respond fast enough," "Losing to other agents"
   - Accept: Any clear pain point
   - Decline: "Nothing really, just curious"

5. **"Are you willing to integrate this with your CRM and provide feedback for 14 days?"**
   - Must be: Yes

## 🚨 Common Objections & Responses

### "I don't get enough leads to justify this"
**Response:** "That's actually perfect for the pilot — we'll make sure you capture 100% of the leads you DO get. Most agents find they're losing 30-40% of their existing leads to slow response. Let's track your current response rate for a week and see."

### "I already have an ISA"
**Response:** "Great! ISAs are awesome. This isn't meant to replace them — it's for the leads that come in when your ISA is off, or to handle overflow when they're busy. Many agents use both — the AI handles instant response, ISA handles the complex conversations."

### "I like responding to my own leads"
**Response:** "Totally get that. This isn't about replacing you — it's about being there when you CAN'T be. The 10pm Zillow lead while you're at dinner. The 6am Sunday inquiry while you're sleeping. You still get every conversation. You just don't lose leads when you're busy."

### "AI sounds impersonal"
**Response:** "I thought so too. But the AI is trained to sound like a professional assistant, not a robot. It asks real questions, handles objections, and books appointments. Want to see a sample conversation? I'll send you one."

### "What if it books bad appointments?"
**Response:** "The AI qualifies before booking — budget, timeline, pre-approval status. Plus you set your calendar rules. It only books during times you specify, and you can review and cancel any appointment. You're still in control."

### "I need to think about it"
**Response:** "Totally understand. Here's the thing — the pilot is free for 14 days, no credit card. You can literally try it for 2 weeks, see if it works, and decide. If it doesn't help, you walk away with zero cost. Fair enough?"
