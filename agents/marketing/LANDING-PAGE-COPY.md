## Design Handoff Notes

### Tone Reminders
- Direct, not fluffy. Agents are busy professionals.
- Lead with the pain, solve with the product.
- Numbers drive trust (30s, 78%, 35%, 24/7) — use them prominently.
- "Pilot" and "Limited Spots" create urgency without being fake.

### CTA Hierarchy
1. **Primary:** "Start Your Free 14-Day Trial →" (hero, form, final banner)
2. **Secondary:** "See How It Works ↓" (hero only)
3. **Soft:** "Contact Us" (Team tier only)

### Urgency Elements
- "Pilot Access — Limited to 10 Agents" in hero badge
- "3 spots remaining" (update this number in real time or remove once pilot is full)
- "14-day trial" in every CTA — lower friction than "free forever"

### A/B Test Recommendations (for dev)
Test these headline variants against the primary:
- **Variant A (Current):** "Stop Losing Deals to Slow Response."
- **Variant B (Urgency):** "78% of Deals Go to the First Responder. Are You First?"
- **Variant C (Benefit-forward):** "Respond to Every Lead in 30 Seconds — While You're in a Showing."
- **Variant D (Loss aversion):** "The Lead You Missed Today? That Was an $8,000 Commission."
