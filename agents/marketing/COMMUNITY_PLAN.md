## 🏆 Group Engagement Strategy

### Level 1: Lurk & Learn (Week 1)
- Join all 10 groups
- Read rules and pinned posts
- Observe posting patterns
- Identify group culture and tone
- Note active influencers/responders

### Level 2: Value Contribution (Week 1-2)
- Comment thoughtfully on 3-5 posts/day
- Answer questions from other agents
- Share relevant industry stats
- Build personal credibility
- Never mention your product yet

### Level 3: Content Creation (Week 2-3)
- Begin original posts (value-first)
- Use the 80/20 rule: 80% value, 20% soft pitch
- Tag relevant people when appropriate
- Respond to every comment quickly
- Build relationships with power users

### Level 4: Pilot Recruitment (Week 3-4)
- Direct pilot offers to engaged members
- DM warm prospects who engaged with posts
- Create "insider" feeling for early adopters
- Collect testimonials for social proof
