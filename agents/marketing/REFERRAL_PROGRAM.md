# Referral Program

**Program Name:** Agent Accelerator Program  
**Launch Date:** Week 3 (after pilots convert)  
**Status:** Ready for deployment

---

## 🎯 Program Overview

### The Offer: Give $100, Get $100

| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Refer a new customer | $100 account credit |
| New customer signs up | $100 off their first month |

### Why This Works
- Real estate is inherently referral-based
- Agents trust other agents more than advertising
- $100 is meaningful but not excessive
- Simple to understand and explain
- No limits = unlimited earning potential
