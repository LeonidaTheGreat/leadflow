## 🎯 Hook Formula Reference

### High-Engagement Hooks:
1. **Question format:** "Quick question for the group..."
2. **Blank fill:** "The lead you missed today cost you $______"
3. **Story start:** "3am lead alert. Phone buzzes..."
4. **Hot take:** "Hot take: Response time > Perfect response"
5. **Confession:** "Sunday evening confession..."
6. **Math problem:** "💰 Math problem:"
7. **Behind scenes:** "Building in public: Day 1"
8. **Results:** "Pilot results after 7 days:"
9. **Quote:** ""I was skeptical...""
10. **Urgency:** "5 pilot spots left..."

### Engagement Tactics:
- Always end with a question
- Use emojis sparingly but strategically
- Numbered lists perform well
- Ask for specific comments ("Comment PILOT")
- Create FOMO with scarcity
- Share real numbers/results
- Use "you" and "your" frequently
