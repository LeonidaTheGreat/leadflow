## 4. Organic Acquisition Content Strategy

### Foundational Principles

Based on research of successful AI product launches (Cursor, Gamma, ElevenLabs, Clay):

1. **Demonstrate, Don't Explain** — Show the product working, don't describe features
2. **Teach, Don't Sell** — Educational content builds trust and ranks in search
3. **Consistency > Virality** — Regular quality content beats sporadic viral attempts
4. **Community First** — Build relationships before asking for anything
