## 🛡️ Objection Handling Scripts

### Price Objections

**"It's too expensive"**
```
Let's do the math:

InstantLead AI: $800/month
Average commission per deal: $10,000

If this helps you close just ONE extra deal every 3 months, it pays for itself.

Our pilot agents are closing 2-4 extra deals per month.

So the real question isn't "Can I afford $800/month?"

It's "Can I afford to keep losing $10,000-40,000/month in missed commissions?"
```

**"I can hire an ISA for that price"**
```
True! An ISA costs $3,000-5,000/month plus benefits, training, and management time.

InstantLead AI is $800/month. No benefits. No training. No management. Works 24/7.

An ISA responds during business hours. Our AI responds at 10pm Saturday while you're at dinner.

Most agents who "have an ISA" still lose 30% of their after-hours leads.

This isn't either/or. Many agents use both. The AI handles instant response, the ISA handles 
complex conversations.
```

### Product Objections

**"AI sounds too robotic"**
```
I get that concern. Let me send you an actual conversation our AI had with a lead.

[Share sample conversation]

Sounds like a professional assistant, right? That's because it's trained on thousands of real 
estate conversations.

It asks about budget, timeline, pre-approval. It handles "I need to think about it" and "I'm 
just looking."

The lead doesn't know it's AI. They just know they got a fast, helpful response.
```

**"What if it books bad appointments?"**
```
Great question. Here's how we prevent that:

1. The AI qualifies BEFORE booking — budget, timeline, pre-approval status
2. You set your calendar rules — only books during times you specify
3. You review and can cancel any appointment
4. It learns from your feedback — flag a bad appointment, it adjusts

You're still in control. The AI just handles the first response and qualification.
```

**"I don't get enough leads to justify this"**
```
I hear this a lot. But here's the thing — this isn't about volume, it's about conversion.

If you get 10 leads/month and lose 3 to slow response, that's $30,000-45,000 in lost commissions.

For $800/month, you could capture all 10 leads. Even if you only close one extra deal every 
2-3 months, you're ahead.

Plus, most agents find they get MORE leads once they have a system in place. You start marketing 
more aggressively because you know you won't lose them.
```

## 📞 Sales Call Script

```
Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. Before I dive into what we do, I want to understand your 
situation better.

Quick question: How are you currently handling lead response? Walk me through what happens when 
a new lead comes in from Zillow or your website.

[Listen — take notes on pain points]

Got it. So [summarize what they said]. 

Here's what I'm hearing: [restate their main challenges]. Does that sound right?
```

### The Pitch (5 minutes)

```
Here's what we built: InstantLead AI responds to your leads in under 30 seconds, has a natural 
conversation to qualify them, and books appointments directly on your calendar.

Let me show you exactly how it works:

[Share screen — show dashboard and sample conversation]

See how it asks about budget, timeline, and pre-approval? And when the lead is qualified, it 
sends them your Cal.com link and books the appointment.

You wake up to qualified appointments. Not just "leads to follow up with."

[Show results from other agents]

This agent was in a similar situation to you. They were getting 15 leads a week but only responding 
to 6 because they were busy. With our AI, they now respond to all 15, and they're booking 4-5 
appointments a week instead of 1-2.

That's the difference between 3 deals a month and 7 deals a month.
```

### Discovery (5 minutes)

```
Based on what you told me about getting [X leads/week], let's run the numbers:

If you're like most agents, you're probably responding to about 60-70% of your leads within a 
reasonable time. The rest are going cold or finding other agents.

commission] = potentially $X,XXX more per month in commissions.

What's your gut reaction to those numbers?
```

### Close (3 minutes)

```
Here's what I recommend: Let's start with a free 14-day pilot.

happens.

Fair enough?

[If yes]: Great! I have time tomorrow at 2pm or Thursday at 10am for a 30-minute setup call.
```
