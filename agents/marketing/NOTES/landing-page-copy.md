## 7. FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions**

### FAQ Items

**Q: Will this sound like a robot?**
A: No. LeadFlow AI is trained specifically on real estate conversations. It uses natural language, asks contextual questions, and adapts based on lead responses. Most leads don't realize they're talking to AI—and agents tell us the conversations feel surprisingly human.

**Q: What if a lead asks something the AI can't answer?**
A: The AI is trained to handle 90%+ of common real estate questions (pricing, neighborhoods, timeline, pre-approval, etc.). For complex questions or urgent situations, the AI can escalate to you immediately with a summary of the conversation so far.

**Q: Is this TCPA compliant?**
A: Yes. LeadFlow AI includes built-in TCPA protections: automatic opt-out handling (leads can text STOP anytime), consent tracking, quiet hours (9 PM - 8 AM local time), and message logging for compliance records. We take legal compliance seriously.

**Q: Do I need to be technical to set this up?**
A: Not at all. If you can use Follow Up Boss, you can use LeadFlow AI. Setup takes about 5 minutes—just connect your FUB account and Cal.com calendar. We handle the rest.

**Q: What happens after the 30-day pilot?**
A: At the end of your pilot, you choose: continue as a paying customer at $49/month (or $39/month with your pilot discount), or cancel with no penalties. No auto-billing surprises. We'll ask for your feedback either way—it helps us improve.

**Q: Can I customize the AI's responses?**
A: During the pilot, we use proven conversation templates optimized for real estate. Post-pilot, you'll be able to customize tone, add your own qualifying questions, and inject your personality into responses.

**Q: What CRMs do you integrate with?**
A: Currently we integrate with Follow Up Boss (FUB). If you're using a different CRM, let us know—we're expanding integrations based on pilot agent feedback.
