## Objection Handling

**"I already use an ISA."**
> "That's great — what's your response time? If they're getting to leads in under 30 seconds 24/7, you're set. If not, LeadFlow could fill the gaps and handle nights/weekends without extra payroll."

**"I don't have time to learn a new tool."**
> "That's exactly why I do the setup for you. You don't learn anything. I connect it, you see it working, and then we decide if it's worth keeping. The only thing you do is approve."

**"Is this TCPA compliant?"**
> "Yes — built for it. A2P 10DLC registered, opt-out in every message, and we follow TCPA guidelines on messaging timing and consent."

**"How is this different from what FUB already does?"**
> "FUB is your CRM — it stores leads. LeadFlow responds to them instantly in under 30 seconds, via SMS, with a real AI conversation. FUB doesn't do that. We work alongside it, not instead of it."

**"What happens to my data?"**
> "Your data stays in FUB — we just read new leads and send SMS responses. Nothing is stored outside your existing systems except the conversation logs in your dashboard."
