# Product Executive - Research Notes

## Key Market Insights (2026)

### AI Agents Market
- Projected growth: $8.03 billion (2025) → $11.78 billion (2026)
- 46% YoY growth rate
- Hyper-automation market: $249 billion by 2032

### High-Opportunity Categories

#### 1. AI Automation Micro-Agency
- **Target**: Small businesses (local dentists, coaches, trades)
- **Model**: Done-for-you automation setup + monthly retainers
- **Pricing**: $2-20k setup + $500-3k/month retainers
- **Potential**: $5k-50k/month per agency
- **Why Now**: SMBs know they need AI but can't hire full-time

#### 2. AI Content Repurposing Studio
- **Target**: Content creators, businesses with video/audio content
- **Model**: Turn long-form content into clips, posts, newsletters
- **Pricing**: $500-2k/month per client
- **Potential**: $2k-10k/month per seat
- **Why Now**: Creator economy exploding, repurposing is tedious

#### 3. AI Lead Response/Management Systems
- **Target**: Real estate, home services, professional services
- **Model**: Instant AI response to inquiries + qualification + booking
- **Pricing**: $1k-3k/month
- **Why Now**: Speed-to-lead is critical; 5min delay = 80% drop in conversion

#### 4. Industry-Specific AI SaaS
- **Target**: Vertical markets (dental, legal, HVAC, etc.)
- **Model**: Niche AI tools that solve specific workflows
- **Pricing**: $99-499/month per seat
- **Potential**: High MRR with 100-200 customers
- **Why Now**: Horizontal tools don't solve vertical problems

#### 5. AI Workforce Training/Enablement
- **Target**: Companies upskilling employees on AI tools
- **Model**: Training programs + ongoing support
- **Pricing**: $5k-25k engagements
- **Why Now**: Massive skills gap; companies need help adopting AI
