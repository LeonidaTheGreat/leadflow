### Page 2: The 3-Part Response System

**Headline:** The 3-Part Response System That Converts

**Body Copy:**

Top agents don't just respond fast—they respond *smart*. Here's the framework:

**PART 1: Immediate Acknowledgment (0-2 minutes)**

The goal: Stop the shopping. Make them feel heard.

Example:
> "Hi [Name]! This is [Agent Name] with [Brokerage]. I just saw your message about [property/interest] and wanted to reach out immediately. I'm pulling some options for you right now—can you tell me your timeline?"

**Why it works:**
- Personal (uses their name)
- Fast (shows you're responsive)
- Asks a question (keeps conversation going)
- Sets expectation (you're working on it)

**PART 2: Value Delivery (2-5 minutes)**

The goal: Prove you're the expert they need.

Follow up with something useful:
- 2-3 listings that match their criteria
- A market report for their area
- Answers to common questions
- A link to book a call

Example:
> "Here are 3 homes that just hit the market in [Area] this week. Two have open houses this weekend. Want me to send you the details?"

**PART 3: Clear Next Step (5-10 minutes)**

The goal: Move from "chatting" to "meeting."

Every conversation needs a destination. Options:
- "When's a good time for a 10-minute call this week?"
- "I'm showing properties Saturday afternoon—want to join?"
- "Here's my calendar: [link] — pick a time that works."

**The Rule:** Never end a conversation without scheduling the next touchpoint.
